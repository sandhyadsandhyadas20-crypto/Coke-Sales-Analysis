# Coke-Sales-Analysis
This project presents an in-depth sales analysis of Coca-Cola beverages across various U.S. regions for the year 2021. The objective was to explore sales performance, identify trends, and generate insights that support strategic business decisions.

The analysis covers data cleaning, transformation, and visualization stages, progressing from beginner-friendly Excel operations to advanced analytical insights.

Highest Sales: The Southern region contributed the most to overall sales.

Trend Observation: Seasonal spikes were noted during summer months, suggesting targeted promotional campaigns could enhance revenue.
